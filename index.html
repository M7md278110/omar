If you want to walk through some amazing places in Egypt, the Western Desert is for you. It takes 150 miles to walk and no one can travel this distance.

The Western Desert is a desert that extends from northern Egypt to southern Egypt, and there are many hills in it that enjoy beauty and scenic views.

The road passes through many valleys, reserves and oases, as it is home to some wildlife, the first of which are camels, jerboas, snakes and scorpions. Do not worry about getting lost if you have a map or Gps system

Most of the people reside in the Siwa oasis in the northwest, while others reside in the Kharga oases in the south.
